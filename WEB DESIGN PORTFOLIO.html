Project 3: The Art of Attack On Titan

This presentation is about my view of the art of the anime Attack On Titan. The art styles used in the anime that I discuss are manga, animation, and music. I feature videos and audios in my presentation to really show my passion for my favorite anime and how much work went into its creation.
